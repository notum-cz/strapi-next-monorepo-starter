## Appendix: Methodology

### Syntropic Agriculture

Based on Ernst Götsch's work, syntropic agriculture mimics natural forest succession to create abundant food forests that improve soil health, sequester carbon, and increase biodiversity.

### Permaculture Design

Using permaculture ethics (Earth Care, People Care, Fair Share) and principles to design integrated, sustainable systems.

### Community Building

Consensus decision-making, nonviolent communication, and restorative justice practices.
